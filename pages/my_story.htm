Points to note for creating a spell:-

1) A magic spell should have a beginning and an ending.
2) I use the standard "As I will it" as the beginning of the spell and end it with "So shall it be".
3) Always use proper grammar, I used to think that magic is just will power and the desire to bring a change that makes it happen, I was so wrong, always pay attention to grammar; English language and modern languages were most likely made by occultists otherwise my spells couldn't have backfired. My intension is always clear, but my spells have had some unexpected results due to bad grammar and formatting.
4 Never use negative words, if you don't know how to use them, a spell can only backfire if you use words like death in the wrong context. Always be careful of negative words or don't use them at all.
5 Use the word shall, instead of should and would because shall make things happen. The word die is more direct and powerful than death because death implies that something or someone who used to be alive is no more; which slightly misses the action from the spell and the spell relies on other words for better context.
